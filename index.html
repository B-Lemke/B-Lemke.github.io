Broderick Lemke;

21st Century Creative

A Jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one.

Broderick Lemke is a native of the Fox Valley region of North East Wisconsin. He has a Bachelor's degree in Music Liberal Arts, and has a passion for anything related to art. He has had musical works performed by several local ensembles, and continues to create new works in both the visual and musical arts. As his most recent challenge, he has chosen to pursue a Software Development Associate's degree from Fox Valley Technical College. He is looking forward to harnessing his creativity in new ways and creating digital content that explores relationships between technology and the arts.
